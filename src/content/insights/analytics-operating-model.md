## Executive summary
As analytics programs mature, leaders need clarity on ownership, data governance, and decision rights. A strong operating model creates consistency while keeping teams close to the business.

## Key considerations
- Define a single source of truth for critical metrics.
- Clarify how data requests are prioritized and funded.
- Establish a cadence for quality reviews and metric changes.

## What to do next
Start with a short diagnostic that maps ownership, tooling, and decision-making. Then set a roadmap that aligns analytics work to executive goals.
