## Executive summary
Automation programs need guardrails that protect risk, security, and compliance. The goal is to create fast review cycles, not bottlenecks.

## Core guardrails
- A clear intake process with value scoring.
- Security and compliance checkpoints that align to existing reviews.
- Shared templates for ROI, risk, and change management.

## What to do next
Stand up a lightweight automation council and publish a playbook that teams can follow without heavy oversight.
